About Us

MakEatQuick was founded 15 years ago by three individuals named Tyler, Chirag and Chrisitan. The concept was simple, but the initiation was difficult. They wanted a to create something that had the quality of a 5 star restaurant, but the price and speed of a fast food joint. To accomplish this they had to deal with many perrials along their way, but after a long journey of nearly 3 founding years, the three were finally able to realize their dream. In the year 2012 on Feb 2nd, MakEatQuick was born. Since then, MakEatQuick has quickly grew in stature, and has hit an all time high in demand as new store branches are opening up across the country and abroad. At MakEatQuick, we stick to our founders ideals, and so when you eat with us, you can expect nothing but the best in speed, quality and price!
